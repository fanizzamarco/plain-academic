My research addresses questions in quantum statistical inference and quantum communication, with focus on:

  • quantum statistical learning theory, quantum property testing and estimation, sequential analysis,
  • characterization and learning of finitely correlated states,
  • capacities of quantum channels, both in discrete and continuous variables,
  • mathematical methods from group representation theory, convex geometry, operator systems, concentration inequalities.
